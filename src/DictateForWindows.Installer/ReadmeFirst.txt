DICTATE FOR WINDOWS
Voice dictation with AI-powered transcription and rewording

=============================================================

REQUIREMENTS
------------
- Windows 10 version 1809 or later (Windows 11 recommended)
- An API key from OpenAI or Groq for transcription services
- A working microphone

FIRST-TIME SETUP
----------------
1. After installation, launch Dictate from the Start Menu
2. The onboarding wizard will guide you through configuration
3. Enter your OpenAI or Groq API key
4. Configure your preferred hotkey (default: Win+Shift+D)

HOW TO USE
----------
1. Press the configured hotkey (Win+Shift+D) to open Dictate
2. Click the record button or press the hotkey again to start recording
3. Speak clearly into your microphone
4. Press the hotkey or click stop to finish recording
5. Your transcribed text will be inserted at the cursor position

FEATURES
--------
- Voice transcription using OpenAI Whisper or Groq
- AI-powered text rewording with custom prompts
- Support for 60+ languages
- Bluetooth microphone support
- Usage tracking and cost estimation
- Light and dark theme support
